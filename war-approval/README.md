# War Approval Ratings · 1983–2024

Interactive chart showing public approval of major military conflicts within the first 1–2 weeks of hostilities, sourced from Gallup, Pew Research, YouGov, Eurobarometer, and other major polling organisations.

## Conflicts covered

- **Grenada** (1983) — US
- **Panama** (1989) — US
- **Gulf War** (1991) — US, UK
- **Kosovo** (1999) — US, UK, France
- **Afghanistan** (2001) — US, UK
- **Iraq War** (2003) — US, UK
- **Libya** (2011) — US, UK, France
- **ISIS / Syria-Iraq** (2014) — US, UK, France
- **Ukraine / Western support** (2022) — US, UK, France, EU avg
